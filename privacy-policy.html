Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

At Age Master, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our age calculator service.

1. Information We Collect

Age Master is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Date and Time of Birth: This information is processed locally in your browser and is NOT stored on our servers.
  • Usage Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (page views, time spent, etc.).
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics and to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your birth date information is used solely to:

  • Calculate your exact age in real-time
  • Display life metrics and insights
  • Show celebrity birthday twins via Wikipedia API

Important: All calculations are performed in your browser. We do not transmit or store your birth date on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Wikipedia API: To fetch celebrity birthday information
  • Google Fonts: To display custom typography
  • Google Analytics: To understand site usage (if enabled)
  • Google AdSense: To display relevant advertisements

4. Data Security

Since we don't store your personal information on our servers, there's minimal risk of data breaches. Your birth date remains private and is only processed locally in your browser.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your browser data at any time
  • Opt-out of analytics cookies
  • Request information about data we may have collected

6. Children's Privacy

Age Master is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all processing happens locally in the browser, no data is transmitted to our servers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
