Frequently Asked Questions

How is my exact age calculated?

Age Master uses high-resolution temporal offsets to compute the exact difference between your birth moment and the current time. We account for leap years, varying month lengths, and time zone variables to ensure 100% precision.

What are celebrity birthday twins?

Birthday twins are famous personalities who were born on the same month and day as you. We leverage the Wikimedia API to fetch verified historical and contemporary figures to show you who shares your special day.

How does the life progress bar work?

The life journey progress bar is a philosophical tool that visualizes your age against a standard 80-year human lifespan. It serves as a reminder of the value of time and the miracle of your ongoing journey on planet Earth.
